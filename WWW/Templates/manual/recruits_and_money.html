Manual: Recruits & Money

Recruits

At the start of every turn, players are given new units, to assign to their Bases.
Any recruits that cannot be assigned to a Base due to capacity restrictions are lost.
1 recruit is given for every 5 land squares a player owns (after modifiers).

Money

At the start of every turn, players are given money. Money is used to buy expansions and effects, etc.
1 money is given for every unit owned (after modifiers).

View

Players can only see adjacent squares to occupied squares.
Bases and Forts can see 2 squares in each direction.
Towers that are occupied can see 4 squares in all directions.
Squares that cannot been seen will show land type only.
