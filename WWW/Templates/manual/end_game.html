Manual: Ending Games

Losing

A player loses when one of the following conditions are met:
  • They lose there last base
  • They own less than 5 squares
  • They resign

Winning

The idea of the game is to meet a winning condition, these are:
  • Having more than 1 million money
  • Owning more than 50% of occupyable area (Bridges count)
  • Having a population of more that 1000
  • Last player in the game
  • Person with the highest score after maximum game turns
The values can be changed from the defaults when a player sets the game up.

Scoring

If the game ends due to the maximum number of turns elapsing, then the winner is decided by scoring.
