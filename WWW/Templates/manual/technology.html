Manual: Technology

 Technology can be researched at School, University and Research Center expansions, each technology takes a different number of technology points, the number of points earned each turn is based on the number of Schools, University and Research Center a player has and also the number of units on those expansions after all attacks.
All players generate 1 technology point per turn.
For each School the player owns they receive 1 technology point.
For each University the player owns they receive 2 technology points.
For each Research Center the player owns they receive 3 technology points.
For each unit on a School the player owns they receive 1/9th of a technology point.
For each unit on a University the player owns they receive 2/9ths of a technology points.
For each unit on a Research Center the player owns they receive 3/9ths of a technology points.
The total whole technology points is the amount of research for that player, that turn.
Note: Partial technology points are lost
Note: The technology points cost will change as balance testing is performed
