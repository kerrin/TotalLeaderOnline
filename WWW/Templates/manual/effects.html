Manual: Square Effects

 Squares can have effects on them, these can be initiated by players for various reasons or randomly occur.
Square Effects
Square EffectEffect on squareCost to Player to Create

Storm-1 unit in battles to both sides, may damage expansions, may move to adjacent squares, regardless of ownerRandomly Occuring
ScoutScout owner has full view of square for number of turns equal to units sent, owner of square gains a unit per turn for this duration1 to 9 Units
Suicidal ScoutScout owner has full view of square for number of turns equal to units sent1 to 9 Units, Plus Money
Kamikaze ScoutScout owner has full view of square for number of turns equal to units sent, owner of square losses a unit per turn for the duration1 to 9 Units, Plus Money
Plague-1 unit in battle. Lose one unit per turn for number of turn equal to number of units that initiated it1 to 9 Units, Plus Money
Celebration+1 unit in battle, counts as two squares for recruiting and doubles unit count for money calculationsMoney
