Manual: Board

The board is made up of a grid of 'Squares'. Note: 'Squares' is used to refer to a single segment of the board that can be owned by one person.
A square starts of as either land or water.
Land squares have different properties, e.g. mountains, forests, plains, etc.
Natives (non-player units) of varied amounts occupy all land squares.
No square can ever contain more than 9 units.
Players can move units to adjacent squares they own, or attack adjacent squares they don't yet own. Different square types have different attributes.
Square TypeProperty
SeaPassable with ships from Ports and by building Bridges
PlainsNormal
Mountain+1 to attack to, -1 to attack from
Forest+1 to attack to
The squares alternate between major squares (hexigons) and minor squares (square).

Major Square Minor Square
 

Only major squares can have Expansions, and can be got to from all 8 directions.
The minor squares can only be got to from the 4 adjacent major squares, so are easier to defend, but cannot have improvements.
