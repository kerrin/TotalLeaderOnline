The Board

The board is made up of a grid of 'Squares'. Note: 'Squares' is used to refer to a single segment of the board that can be owned by one person.
A square is either land or water. Land squares have different properties, i.e. mountains, forests, and plains.
Natives (non-player units) of varied amounts occupy all land squares.

Once the game has started, you will not be able to see the number of units on a square, or ownership of any squares beyond your line of sight, which is usually the adjacent squares to ones you own, so make sure you look at the board while you can.

If you wish to know more on the board layout, look in the appropriate section of the Manual
