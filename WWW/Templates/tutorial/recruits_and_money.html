Recruits & Money

At the end of every turn, players are given new recruits and money.

The number of recruits is based on the amount of land they own. For every 5 squares owned, you will get a recruit.

The amount of money you get is based on the number of units owned.

Both of these numbers can be increased by effects and expansions.

To see how much money and how many recruits have been trained ready for assignment, you can view the Summary screen by selecting the Summary button on the left menu.

For a more detailed explaination of recruits and money look in the appropriate section of the Manual
