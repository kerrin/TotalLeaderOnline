Expansions

You can not build any expansions until you have finished researching a technology that allows you to build one.

We started off researching Teaching in turn 1, so on turn 6 we will be able to build schools.

On turn 6, make sure you have 9 units on a major land square that doesn't already have an expansion on.
Instead of moving these units, we are going to put them to work building a school.
View the square they are on, and the move drop down will now have an additional option 'Build School'.
Change the drop down to school and press the Move button.
All 9 of the units are now building the school and cannot be used for anything else, including defending this square.

A School take 2 turns to build. So on the next turn, the units will still be busy building, and a school building will appear on the square, marked with a building in progress symbol.

Once the school is finsihed the building in progress symbol will disappear and the school will now be producing addtional research points.

By keeping 9 units on this school, you will gain an addtional science point, so it is worth considering leaving 9 units on the school, plus they will defend the school from attacking forces.

To find out what the expansions are, and what each of their benifits are, look in the appropriate section of the Manual
