Moving Around The Board

You can move around the board in two ways:

  • Pressing a green arrow at the edge of the board (, , , ) to move the view of the board in that direction.
  • Making sure the drop down box at the top of the screen says 'Move View' and then selecting a square by pressing the left mouse button; this will center the view on the selected square.
