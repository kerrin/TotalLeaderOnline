How To Use This Tutorial

This tutorial will take you through a step by step on how to find, join, and start to play a game. The Manual has a detailed list of how the game works.

At anytime you can see which section you are in by looking at the menu bar above. The current section is a different colour. You can also jump straight to any part of the tutorial by pressing the section name

If you wish to know more on any area of the game, simply look in the appropriate section of the Manual

You are now ready to start using the tutorial, so lets begin the tour. Press the Next button to continue.
