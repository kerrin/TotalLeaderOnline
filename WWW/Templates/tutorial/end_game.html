End of Game

The game can end for a player in a number of ways:

  1. Someone wins
  2. They lose their last Base (Without a base, you can't assign new recruits)
  3. They own less than 5 squares
  4. The player resigns
  5. The game ends as the maximum number of turns is reached
When A player loses (2, 3 and 4), their units become natives.

A player can win the game when:

  • They have 1 million in money
  • They own 1/2 of the land squares
  • They have 1000 units
  • All other players have lost
  • They are the person with the highest score when the maximum number of turns is reached

For more details on the end of the game, including a breakdown on how scoring is calculated, view the appropriate section of the Manual
